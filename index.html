About

The Space Y Project is a new Web3 platform where humans can earn money while improving life on Earth for all generations.

You can help in different sectors - clear the atmosphere, create drinking water or gather sources of energy.

Atmosphere

We want to plant trees and invest in agriculture.

In this way, we produce healthy food for humans, take care of the soil and make our planet greener getting a cleaner atmosphere as a result.

Water

Humans need drinkable water to live and in some places of the planet that’s no longer possible. With this project, we want to create an affordable filtration process to make ocean water drinkable.

Energy

Our mothership - Earth, has an infinite source of power on its core called Geothermal energy. We want to take advantage of that source and make it available to everyone!

Currency

Anyone who wants to join the project will volunteer their working hours.

In the end, those hours will be converted into EARTH coins making it possible to make a living by helping the planet.

Don’t know how you can help us?
